Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI improve search results?

Our AI technology analyzes the content, context, and metadata of media files to understand what they contain. This allows for more accurate search results based on natural language queries, visual similarity, and semantic understanding rather than just keywords.

What file types are supported?

We support a wide range of file formats:

  • Images: JPG, PNG, GIF, WEBP, SVG, HEIC
  • Video: MP4, MOV, AVI, WEBM, MKV
  • Audio: MP3, WAV, OGG, M4A, FLAC
  • Documents: PDF, DOCX, TXT, MD
How can I improve my search results?

To get better search results:

  • Use specific, descriptive queries
  • Try using synonyms if you don't find what you need
  • Utilize filters to narrow down results
  • Upload a reference image for visual similarity search
  • Add detailed descriptions when uploading your own media
Is there a limit to how much I can upload?

Free accounts have a 5GB storage limit. Premium accounts get 100GB of storage, and Business accounts have 1TB. You can check your current usage in your account settings.
